About Stock Portfolio Tracker

Our Mission

We aim to provide a simple yet powerful tool for investors to track and manage their stock portfolios effectively. Our platform helps you make informed investment decisions by providing real-time portfolio insights.

Features

  • Real-time stock price tracking
  • Portfolio value calculation
  • Performance monitoring
  • Gain/loss tracking
  • Easy stock management
  • Responsive design for all devices

Security

Your portfolio data is stored locally on your device using secure browser storage. We don't collect or store any personal information, ensuring your investment data remains private and secure.

Future Plans

We're constantly working to improve our platform. Upcoming features include:

  • Historical performance charts
  • Portfolio diversification analysis
  • Stock news integration
  • Custom alerts and notifications
